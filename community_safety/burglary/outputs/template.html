Burglary

What is burglary?

Burglary offences involve a person entering a house or other building with the intention of stealing.

Burglaries last month

Figure 1: Count of burglary by month in Trafford
Download image | Download data
There were 137 burglaries recorded by Greater Manchester Police in Trafford during November 2017. This is 16 fewer crimes than the same month last year.

Burglaries over the last 3 years

Figure 2: Trend and seasonality in observed values
Download image | Download data
Burglary offences recorded over the last three years exhibit a clear trend and seasonal variation. Offences peaked in December 2016 with 192 offences and stabilised since then at a level higher than during the preceding two years. At a monthly level burglary tends to peaks in December and January and drop in September.

Burglary rates

Figure 3: Rates of burglary in Trafford's wards
Download image | Download data
There were approximately 1.4 burglaries recorded for every 1,000 Trafford households during November 2017. However, there is some variation in rates of burglary between wards. Brooklands ward recorded the highest rate of burglary with 3.7 offences per 1,000 households.
